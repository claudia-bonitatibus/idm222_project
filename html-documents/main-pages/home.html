Claudia Bonitatibus

About Claudia

Claudia is a second-year student majoring in Interactive Digital Media at Drexel University. A Philadelphia native, having lived her entire life in Center City, she is a big fan of the liveliness, pace, and diversity of the urban environment. She is a graduate of the Philadelphia School and the Science Leadership Academy, a project-based public magnet high school partnered with the Franklin Institute.

Claudia has been active in her community and believes in the importance of supporting citizen programs. She has volunteered in many walks and runs to raise funds for important charitable causes.

As design is her passion, Claudia enjoys drawing, designing patterns, origami, and constructing jewelry. She currently is working part time as a CAD draftsman at Bonitatibus Architects and just completed a Drexel-sponsored architectural study tour in Rome requiring extensive field sketches in both pencil and watercolor media.
