Power Drill Model

Created in the Winter of 2016

This three dimensional model of a powerdrill shows the effect of computer generated imagery and how it interacts with a real world setting.

The drill was modeled in Maya and the textures and bump-map were made using a combination of Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The model was rendered using RenderMan and superimposed into a photograph.
