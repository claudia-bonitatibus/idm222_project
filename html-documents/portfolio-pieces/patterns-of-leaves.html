Patterns of Leaves

Created in the Winter of 2016

This black and white design consists of a repeated leaf motif demonstrating positive and negative space. This piece shows how a single design can have a distinct effect when it is applied as a building block of a more complex composition.

The original pattern was constructed out of equal parts white, black, and middle gray paper. This pattern was later brought into Photoshop and rearranged to form a larger design.
