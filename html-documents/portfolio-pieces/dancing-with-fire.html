Dancing With Fire

Created in the Summer of 2015

This piece was designed to show how several seperate photos can be joined into one composition. The numerous layers with various levels of opacity bring a subtle richness to the final design and contribute to there being a different narrative in the final design than there were in the original photographs.

The photographs were taken with a DSLR camera, edited in Adobe Lightroom, and overlayed in Photoshop.
