Philadelphia Streets Redesign

Created in the Fall of 2017

This is a visually oriented prototype of a redesigned recycling portion of Philadelphia Streets' website. The prototype is designed to display heavy text-based content through graphics which engage and make the information more accessible to the viewer.

The varrious screens and infographics were created in Adobe Illustrator and some adjustments were made in Photoshop. The screens were then taken to invision and made interactive.
