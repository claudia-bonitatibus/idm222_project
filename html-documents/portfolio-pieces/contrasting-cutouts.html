Contrasting Cutouts

Created in the Spring of 2017

This piece was composed using different arrangements and visual adjustments to the hue and shade of the same patern which consists of two complementary colors. Both of the colors take up an equal amount of the visual space.

The pattern was made using color aid paper and the final design was assembled and manipulated in Adobe Photoshop.
