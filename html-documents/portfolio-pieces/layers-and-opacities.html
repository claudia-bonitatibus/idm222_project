Layers and Opacities

Created in the Spring of 2017

This GIF shows how different colors interact with each other by using a several layers of colorful shapes with varying levels of transparency. Altering the opacities of each of the shapes throughout the GIF emphasizes the visual impact of different levels of opacity and gives the interaction between the layers a more dynamic connection.

The vector artwork and the frames were composed in Adobe Illustrator and the GIF was compiled using Photoshop's animation timeline feature.
