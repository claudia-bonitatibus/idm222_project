Painted Archway

Created in the Fall of 2017

This painting depicts Via Giulia in Rome, Italy. It is one of many pieces that were painted during a study abroad trip covering Interior and Architectural Design.

The painting started as a pencil sketch to accurately capture the perspective of the scene and the color was added later using a acquarella style of watercoloring.
