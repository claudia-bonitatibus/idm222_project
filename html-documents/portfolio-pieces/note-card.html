Note Card

Created in the Fall of 2016

This note card is one of two in a series that use soft colors accompanied by delicate curvilinear art deco style motifs which are used to activate the negative space and replicate the curved cutouts of the border.

The note card's design consists of vector artwork which was created using Adobe Illustrator.
