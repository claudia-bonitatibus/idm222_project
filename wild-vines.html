Wild Vines

Created in the Fall of 2013

This design is a mosaic style illustration of surreal vines against a brick red background. The use of white space and black thin lines visually separates the different elements of the composition, and the shading also contributes to making different portions of the design stand out.

This design was made with a series of prismacolor pencils as well as a felt tipped pen.
