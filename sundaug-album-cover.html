Empyrean Fantasy

Created in the Summer of 2013

This pastel illustration of a sundog acts as a piece of album artwork. This project involved a good deal of client work, since the artwork was intended to match the vission of the musical artist and the overall tone of the music itself.

The cover artwork was made using chalk pastels and watercolor. The malleable quality of the pastels allows the piece to have a softer, more fluid, and slightly hazy appearance.
